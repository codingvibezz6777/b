Our Commitment to Privacy

At StarBooking, we understand that privacy is paramount, especially for the high-profile individuals and exclusive clients we serve. We are committed to maintaining the highest levels of confidentiality and security for all personal information entrusted to us.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data across our platform. By using StarBooking, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Identification: Legal names, stage names, and professional titles.
  • Contact Information: Professional email addresses, phone numbers, and agency representation details.
  • Financial Data: Secure payment processing details for transactions and deposits.
  • Booking Logistics: Travel preferences, dietary requirements, and specific technical riders.

2. Use of Data

Your information is used strictly to facilitate the premium booking experience provided by StarBooking. This includes:

Platform Excellence

Personalizing your dashboard and talent suggestions based on historical interactions.

Secure Transactions

Ensuring all financial exchanges between clients and talent are protected and compliant.

3. Data Protection & Security

verified_user Elite Security Standard

We employ bank-grade AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our servers are housed in Tier 4 data centers with 24/7 biometric security and physical surveillance. Access to client and talent data is strictly limited to authorized personnel via multi-factor authentication.

4. Third-Party Sharing

StarBooking does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third-party marketing firms. Information is only shared with verified partners strictly necessary for your booking, such as logistics providers (luxury transport, private aviation) or specific agency representatives involved in the transaction.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and ensure platform stability. Analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with StarBooking to improve our UI/UX. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some premium features may be limited if essential cookies are disabled.
